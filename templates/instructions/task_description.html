This is instruction for the task.

Thank you for participating in this study. In this study, we measure the quality of the search system for shoes, when the user has a concept in mind. You will have 5 search sessions, in which you will look for shoes that are similar to the shown one. To help us, you will proceed a few steps.

Step 1: Introduction

To avoid bias in our results we first ask you to provide some information about yourself.

Step 2: The situation

In the second step, we describe you the hypothetical situation in which this system can be used.

Step 3: Describe and remember

We show you the shoe. You are asked to describe and to remember it. In the next steps, you will try to find a similar shoe.

Step 4: Find the match

We continuously show you different shoes. Please choose ones that match by color or shape, individual or by column. Press the "Buy" button if you find the one. In the last step, you will describe the difference between the best match and the desired shoe.
